A Student-Led Global Community

Welcome to the page of the Model United Nations Team at the International Academy in Bloomfield Hills! We are glad to see you interested in participating in this fantastic club. Model UN will help you become a better educated, globally-minded individual while allowing you to make friends from around the world. Please feel free to use this page as a resource to help guide you on your path to becoming a stronger delegate and improving your Model United Nations skills. On our website, you will find an array of tools such as conference information, preparation tips, and contact details.

Why It Matters

Understanding the World Beyond Headlines

In a world shaped by complex global issues, understanding geopolitics is more important than ever. Model United Nations gives students the opportunity to go beyond surface-level knowledge and actually engage with the forces that influence international decisions. From climate policy to global conflict, MUN challenges us to think critically, consider multiple perspectives, and develop informed opinions on real-world problems.

📚

Research

You learn how to dig deep into topics, analyze sources, and understand issues from multiple angles — not just memorize facts.

🌐

Awareness

MUN builds global awareness by exposing you to different countries, perspectives, and the realities behind international decisions.

🗣️

Advocacy

You develop confidence in speaking, debate, and defending ideas — learning how to advocate for solutions effectively.
