Conference Circuit

Throughout the year, IAC Model UN participates in some of the most prestigious conferences across the Midwest.

MUNIAC

Our home conference, entirely student-run, where we bring together delegates for a dynamic and competitive Model UN experience.

SEMMUNA

A regional conference hosted in Southeast Michigan, known for its engaging committees and collaborative debate environment.

CASC

Held at the Michigan State Capitol Building in Lansing from December 5–6, CASC offers a unique experience debating in a real legislative setting.

MUNUM

Hosted at the University of Michigan from January 15–18, MUNUM provides an immersive collegiate conference atmosphere.

MUNUC

Taking place at the University of Chicago from February 5–8, MUNUC is one of the most prestigious high school Model UN conferences.

MSUMUN

Held at Michigan State University from March 13–15, MSUMUN is a fast-paced conference known for strong competition and engaging committees.
